april - may 2026

onvijandige architectuur (unhostile architecture)

you can read the text here (in dutch & english. i hope the translation makes sense). graphic design project.

background info a manifesto poster against hostile architecture. the poster is cut up. in the first one you CAN read it, it's just a bit harder. like you can sleep sitting up on a shitty park bench or under a bridge, it just takes "more effort", as some might put it. cutting and putting the poster up like this makes it more fragile. the second version has a big circle cut out the middle of the poster, making it illegible.
