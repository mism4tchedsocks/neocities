1. What's your name? How old are you? How would you describe yourself?

oliver, 18, quiet and enthusiastic.
2. What was your upbringing like?

i grew up with both my parents and two younger siblings. both sets of grandparents lived close by so i saw them often as well. i was quiet and respectful ("a joy to have in the classroom!") and mostly kept to myself, which meant my relationship to my parents wasn't super duper in my early teens. now its pretty good. i get along with my brother pretty well, and on some days with my sister as well. she's only 12 though, so i think it'll only get better as she ages.
