alice in wonderland

the first musical i ever saw, was k3: alice in wonderland. i was 3.5 years old at the time, but i still remember most of the lyrics thanks to this really cool invention called DVDs (seriously, why do they barely make those anymore? fucking love dvds). its free to watch on youtube now and the songs are on spotify. idk if its the nostalgia or not, but those songs are absolute bangers. i had a stickerbook, a dress, and one of those shitty balloons that deflate on the same day you buy them. also the costuming??? nicole & hugo???? honestly, my biggest flex is that i saw this live.
fun fact: to this day this is still the only adaptation of alice and wonderland that ive ever seen. i didnt even know that theres usually a cat.
